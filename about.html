The Journey of ASH LAP MART

Founded in 2018, ASH LAP MART started with a simple vision: making high-quality tech accessible to everyone in Sri Lanka. From a small retail shop in Colombo, we have quickly grown into the country's most trusted online & physical retailer for brand new and premium condition second-hand laptops.

We don't just sell computers; we provide solutions. Whether you are a hardcore gamer demanding the ultimate frame rates, a dedicated student managing assignments on a budget, or a corporate professional seeking reliable everyday performance, we ensure that you find the exact match for your requirement.
